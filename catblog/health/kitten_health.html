Quick Flags

  • Urgent No eating/drinking for 24h, severe lethargy, blue/gray gums, difficulty breathing.
  • Soon Vomiting/diarrhea > 24h, eye discharge that persists, sneezing fits, fleas/ticks.
  • Monitor Occasional soft stool, mild sneezes, slight appetite dip for < 12h.

Core Vaccine Timeline

Typical schedule for indoor kittens (varies by region/vet). Always follow your vet’s plan.

  1. 6–8 weeks: FVRCP #1
  2. 10–12 weeks: FVRCP #2
  3. 14–16 weeks: FVRCP #3 & Rabies (per local law)
  4. ~1 year: Boosters
  5. Adult: Vet-guided boosters (1–3 years)
